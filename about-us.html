Dr Nic Williams / Founder, CEO

Dr Nic is a developer’s developer.

He writes for developers; creates tools, libraries and text editor extensions for developers; and speaks to developers at conferences.

He has contributed to almost 300 open source projects. He is known for creating or curating projects such as BOSH, Rails Installer, Composite Primary Keys, Choc Top, App Scrolls, Rails TextMate bundle, New Gem, Tab Tab, GitHub Badges, Install Theme for Rails, and Dr Nic’s Magic Models.

He’s Australian and now living in the Bay Area.

And he’s funny; if you can understand his accent.
